MacAngioView

A DICOM cine-angiogram viewer for macOS. View and play back cardiac catheterization angiography studies from a wide range of cath-lab X-ray systems.

Download for Mac

Free download · macOS

Features

DICOM XA playback

Open and play multi-frame DICOM XA cine-angiogram runs, including lossless-JPEG compressed studies from cardiac catheter X-ray systems.

Broad system compatibility

Reads studies exported from many cath-lab digital imaging systems — load a study CD or exported folder and browse the runs.

Frame-by-frame review

Step through frames, adjust playback speed, and review runs at your own pace.

Export

Export cine runs to standard movie formats for presentations and teaching files.

Note: MacAngioView is intended for review and educational use. It is not certified as a medical device and should not be used for primary diagnosis.

Requirements

  • macOS (Apple silicon or Intel)
  • DICOM XA studies (cine-angiogram runs) on CD, disk, or folder
